About

Gabriela Miranda (2003, Póvoa de Varzim) is a visual artist focused on animation and illustration, with a growing interest in the development of digital games. She holds a degree in Visual Arts – Multimedia from the Faculty of Fine Arts of the University of Porto, where she built a solid foundation in both manual and digital techniques, essential for crafting visual narratives and interactive experiences.

Her work is inspired by a variety of personal interests, including literature, drawing, painting, modeling, and crochet, which she explores through her handmade brand, Crabicanela. These hobbies continuously fuel her creativity, encourage experimentation, and allow her to develop unique visual universes that bring together diverse artistic references and sensibilities.

She is eager to collaborate on innovative projects in animation and interactive media, where she can contribute her artistic vision while further developing her skills in these dynamic fields.
