The Letter

2022

This is a short traditional 2D animation that invites you to pause and reflect. Through a delicate and emotional scene, where a mailbox opens and a letter flies gently to land on the grass, we explore the beauty of movement and the depth of technique. This project seeks to evoke a nostalgic tone, focusing on communication, memory and the emotion linked to simple and tangible gestures. It is a work that contrasts poetically with the digital age, celebrating the serenity and importance of the small but significant moments in life.
