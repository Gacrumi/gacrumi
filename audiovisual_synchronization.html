Audiovisual Synchronization

2023

The animation is a visual exploration of audio synchronization, developed in response to Bruno Coulais's song "Dreaming". The images and movements were created as the musical piece progressed, resulting in an organic fusion of sound and visuals. The work seeks to convey the emotions and dreamlike atmosphere of the composition, inviting the viewer into a sensory immersion and a synaesthetic experience.
